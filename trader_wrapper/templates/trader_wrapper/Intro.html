For this purpose, ECUs you earned will be converted into Canadian Dollars at the rate of X CAD/ECU.   • The experimental interface has two components (i.e., website tabs). The first tab corresponds to a trading platform where you can buy and sell virtual assets. In the second tab, you have the opportunity to perform a simple non-financial task. You can earn ECUs either through profitable stock trades, or by performing non-financial tasks. You can switch between the two components at any point throughout a round.
