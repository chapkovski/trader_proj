Welcome and thank you for participating in the trading experiment!
Please read the instructions carefully. To proceed with the experiment, you will need to correctly answer four validation questions to verify your understanding of the instructions.

What do I need to do?
  1. After completing the validation questions, you will be redirected to a platform where you can:
    • Trade virtual assets (via a Trade tab) and/or
    • Complete simple numerical tasks (via a Work tab)
  2. You can freely switch between the trading and work tabs at all times during the experiment.
  3. The game progresses over {{ gps.num_rounds }} rounds of {{ gps.day_length_in_min }} minutes each.
  4. In each round, you can earn money either by:
    • Making a profit through buying and selling assets.
    • Earning a fixed wage for each numerical task you complete.
  5. After each round, you will see a screen with your total wealth at the end of the round.
  6. The rounds are independent of each other meaning that your progress (portfolio and bank account) will be reset at the beginning of each round.
  7. After the {{ gps.num_rounds }} rounds, you will complete a 10-question quiz on trading and investments.
    Do not worry if you do not have prior finance knowledge or experience in trading: Just answer to the best of your ability.
    You will receive a bonus payment for each question you answer correctly on the quiz.
  8. At the end of the experiment, one of the trading rounds will be selected at random as your payment round.
    You will receive a bonus payment proportional to the wealth in the randomly selected round, at a rate of 1 E$ = {{ gps.real_world_currency_per_point }} CA$ (E$ stands for "experimental dollars"). So think and play in each round as if it is the round that counts, because it might be!
What can I trade?
Each round, you can trade up to four assets:
  1. Two stocks, labelled {{ gps.stock_A_name }} and {{ gps.stock_B_name }} .
    1. Stock {{ gps.stock_B_name }} has a higher expected return (on average, you can anticipate higher gains)
    2. But, stock {{ gps.stock_B_name }} also has a higher volatility (prices swing around more)
  2. Two "leveraged ETFs" which are designed to offer, at each time step, 3 times the return on either {{ gps.stock_A_name }} and {{ gps.stock_B_name }} .
  3. Each round, you start with {{ gps.initial_stock_items }} units each of {{ gps.stock_A_name }} and {{ gps.stock_B_name }}

How do I trade?
  1. Stock prices will refresh every {{ gps.tickFrequency }} seconds.
  2. At the beginning of each round, you will be informed of the expected return and expected variability of returns for each stock.
  3. You cannot take negative positions in a stock, i.e., you cannot short sell.
  4. Each round, a fee might apply for each trade you make (either a buy or a sell). You will be able to see the level of the fee in the trading dashboard.
  5. The trading dashboard allows you to place orders, as well as to see your return and position value in real time, for each stock and across your entire portfolio.
How can I make money through work?
  1. In the work tab, you complete simple numerical tasks to earn a wage of either {{ gps.fee_low }} E$ or {{ gps.fee_high }} E$, depending on the round you are in.
  2. The wage you earn per task will be directly deposited into your bank account balance.
  3. At the top of the screen, you will see the number of total tasks you have attempted, the number correctly done, and the wage you will receive per task.
  4. Remember that it's up to you how to allocate your time between trading and working in each round.
How do I receive payment after the experiment?
  1. Your total compensation includes both a participation fee and a bonus based on your performance in the experiment. Payment is made through the Prolific platform.
  2. At the end of some rounds, you may receive a randomly chosen free stock. The probability of receiving the free stock is 5% times the proportion of time spent on the trading platform. For example, if you spend {{ gps.example_time_min }} minutes on the trading platform and {{ gps.example_work_time_min }} minutes on the working task, you receive one unit of either stock {{ gps.stock_A_name }} or stock {{ gps.stock_B_name }} at the end of the round with probability {{ gps.example_time_min }}/{{ gps.day_length_in_min }} * {{ gps.formatted_prob }}% = {{ gps.example_formatted_prob }}%.
  3. After you are finished, the computer will select one of the trading rounds at random.
  4. This will be your payment round: You will receive a bonus payment proportional to your end-of-round balance (including wages earned and trading profits).
